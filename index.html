Problem Statement

Mobility barriers in everyday environments

Millions of visually impaired individuals face daily mobility challenges due to unexpected obstacles, crowded environments, and limited situational awareness.

Traditional aids provide limited environmental understanding. Percepta addresses this using AI-powered computer vision and intelligent obstacle detection.
